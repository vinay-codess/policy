## 2. Information We Collect

NutriWise AI collects only the minimum data required to operate the Service.

We **do NOT collect**:
- IP addresses  
- Usage analytics  
- Device identifiers  
- Behavioral tracking data  

### 2.1 Personal Data You Provide
We may collect the following information when you voluntarily provide it:
- Email address  
- First and last name (optional)

Used for account creation, login, and customer support.

### 2.2 Images You Upload
With your permission, the Application may access your camera or gallery to process:
- Food label photos  
- Barcode images  

These images are sent to third-party processors (e.g., Microsoft Azure Vision) to generate OCR results.  
Images are **not stored long-term** in our systems unless explicitly stated.  
You may revoke camera/gallery access anytime in your device settings.
